Cathedral

Enter the Cathedral, where musical robots take the stage. This app lets you craft arrangements in an enchanting environment. Featuring artwork designed with stable diffusion, it's a blend of visual and auditory delight.

Crypt

Step into the Crypt, where the necromancer awaits your dance moves! Tap to the rhythm and let the beats guide your steps in this Halloween-themed groove. With pixel art enriched by Retro Diffusion, the crypt becomes a vibrant stage for your dancing delights.

Dance in the Crypt

TITAN

TITAN: where machine learning intersects with music. Use simple hand gestures to play with a range of chords. It's intuitive, responsive, and designed to turn your hand's movement into an auditory experience.

Explore TITAN

Christmas Cat

Join a playful pixelated cat in a festive adventure. Decorate a Christmas tree, but watch out for the occasional mischief. With some friendly mice to assist, it's a fun-filled game for the holiday spirit.
